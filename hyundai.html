Frequently asked questions

How will the payment cycle work?

Payment cycle will be on monthly advance basis. The billing cycle will begin on the mutually agreed date of delivery, and you will need to pay the booking amount (first month subscription fee plus refundable deposit) before handover of the car. This date will also become the due date for subsequent months. Every month, you will receive an invoice that needs to be paid by the due date.

What will be the car registration type and whose name will it be registered in?

The car will be registered as a premium commercial vehicle (black plate) with an All India permit and can be driven anywhere within India without any concerns. It will be registered in the name of Revv (PrimeMover Mobility Technologies Pvt. Ltd.) or its supply partners.
